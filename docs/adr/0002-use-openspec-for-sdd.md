# ADR 0002: Use OpenSpec for SDD

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The project must be recoverable by future Codex sessions and collaborators without chat history.

## Decision

Use OpenSpec changes, specs, and tasks before implementation.

## Alternatives

- README-only planning: too easy to drift.
- Issue tracker only: not available locally to every Codex session.

## Consequences

Every complex change starts with proposal/design/spec/tasks and ends with validation plus archive or explicit unarchived reason.

## Follow-up

Archive `bootstrap-fate-spectrum` after first public demo review or record why it remains active.
